How to Play Vino Bingo

Starting a Game
  1. Choose your game amount (3-200 Birr)
  2. Select a lucky number (1-100)
  3. Get your unique Bingo card
  4. Wait for the game to start (minimum 2 players)
  5. Mark numbers as they're called
  6. First to complete a pattern wins!
